The tfs (Team Foundation Server) supports ssl public key
for checkouts and commits.


1. At http://myhost:8080/tfs:
click User pull-down on upper-right
click My profile
click SSH KEYS on right

2. At USER PROFILE
under YOUR PUBLIC KEYS
click Add an SSH public key
